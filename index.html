STEM the Future

STEM the Future is a student led organization that provides kids of all ages opportunities in the field of STEM through student led workshops across the country

Our mission is to provide accessible and engaging STEM education opportunities for all students, regardless of their background. We want the future leaders of the world to be exposed to the field today.

Your Questions Answered

What is STEM the Future?

STEM the Future is a student led organization dedicated to bringing hands-on STEM activities and workshops to kids across the country.

How can I find a workshop?

You can easily locate a workshop near you by using our search tool or interactive map on the 'Locate a Workshop' page. We're always expanding our reach!

Can I start a new chapter?

Absolutely! We are now accepting applications for new chapters to reach all 50 states by the end of 2025. Just fill out the google form - https://forms.gle/7Zp3Lh6yfhvXojCBA

How can I join the team?

We're always looking for talented and dedicated individuals to join our mission. Check out the 'Join the Team' section for current opportunities - https://forms.gle/7Zp3Lh6yfhvXojCBA

How can I support STEM the Future?

Your support is invaluable! You can get involved by volunteering, forming partnerships with schools or companies, or by making a donation. Every contribution helps us build a brighter future. Reach out to us at stemthefuture1@gmail.com
